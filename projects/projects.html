Time to go Home


For my graduating project, my team crafted a narrative game that touches on neglect, through the whimsical and innocent eyes of a child.
Play as Kipp as he wastes away the hours after school, waiting to go home… fill up the time, drawing, sticking and wandering around in Kipp’s wondrous imagination!
