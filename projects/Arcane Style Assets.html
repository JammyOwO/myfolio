Arcane Style Assets

I modelled and textured 3d assets in the style of the animated series Arcane.


For a university assignment, I was tasked to recreate a scene diorama in the style and sensibility of the animated series 'Arcane', by Riot Games and studio Fortiche. In this assignment I created a number of assets embodying the grim steampunk aesthetic of Zaun, including the creature Rio.


After creating 3D assets, we were tasked with bringing them to life in a game scene diorama. We we're able to use the work of our classmates as well which was shared in a scene Library. I put together the scene below.
